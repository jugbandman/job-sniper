# User Preferences

Style and formatting preferences for generated materials. Customize these to match your voice.

---

## Cover Letter Style

**Length:** 600-700 words
**Tone:** Conversational and authentic, not corporate or sales-y
**Structure:** Natural flow with minimal headers (opening, why this company, what you bring, closing)

**Do:**
- Write like you talk, not like a formal business letter
- Use real company names and specific metrics
- Add personal touches (career context, what excites you, location preferences)
- Show understanding from multiple perspectives (managing + selling, technical + business)
- Be humble but confident ("helped the team" not "single-handedly drove")
- End with personality (something genuine about why you're excited)

**Don't:**
- Pre-emptively defend gaps (lead with strengths instead)
- Over-structure with bold headers and bullets everywhere
- Use generic claims without proof points
- Sound like a marketing brochure

---

## Writing Rules

- No em-dashes. Use commas or parentheses instead.
- No colons in prose. Restructure or use commas. (Colons OK in headers/labels/lists)
- No AI-ey patterns ("Here's the thing...", "not this, that" contrasts, setup-payoff reveals)
- Specific company names, specific metrics, always.
- YYYY-MM-DD date format.

## Research Depth Defaults

| Depth | Use When | Weekly Target |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **Quick** | Qualified, interested | 5-8 applications |
| **Standard** | Strong match, excited | 2-3 applications |
| **Deep** | Dream job, perfect fit | 1 application |

**Default depth:** Quick (override per job as needed)

---

## LinkedIn Message Style

- Short (2-3 sentences max)
- Lead with value (what's in it for them)
- Reference mutual connection or company insight
- Include call to action (suggest a quick chat)

---

## Email Follow-up Cadence

- Day 0: Application + research link (if Deep mode)
- Day 3: Follow-up with a new insight about the company
- Day 7: Bump with additional value or a question

| Tier | Threshold | Meaning |
